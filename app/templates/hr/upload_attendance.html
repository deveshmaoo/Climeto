CSV Format Requirements

Required Columns:

  • employee_id - Employee ID
  • date - Date (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • in_time - Check in time (HH:MM)
  • out_time - Check out time (HH:MM)
  • status - Status (Present/Absent/Late/Half-Day)

Example:

1,2024-01-15,09:00,17:30,Present
2,2024-01-15,09:15,17:30,Late
3,2024-01-15,,,Absent
